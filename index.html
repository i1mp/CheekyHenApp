Cheeky Hen - Cava Tasting

Cava Tasting

From £17.00
3 Review(s)

Cava is officially the coolest fizz on the block! And what better place to learn about this Spanish sparkler and try a selection of Cavas than at the UK’s first dedicated Cava bar in Brighton.
Let the bar staff guide you through different styles of Cava while you learn about how Cava is made, what makes it unique, and the difference between Cava and other Sparkling Wines (Champagne, Prosecco etc).
You will learn how to taste Cava and have some fun with a blind tasting. Vote for your favourite to receive a discount.
And put your new-found knowledge to the test for the chance to win a Cava cocktail.


Sample a selection of 6 different Cavas from our extensive range:

  • Codorníu Brut NV
  • Codorníu Rose Brut NV
  • Codorníu Semi-Seco NV
  • Anna de Codorníu Brut NV* Anna de Codorníu Blanc de Noirs Brut NV
  • Anna De Codorníu Rose Brut NV
